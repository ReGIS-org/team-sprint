Use Case Flagship

Description:
Curse of locality: despite advances in modelling, specific models/data are more informative for a location on the map than more general model/data. Wikipedia voor modellen
Researchers can select local geodata patches and claim it. They thereby state that they have valuable information on that area in the form of a model or dataset. A Common Vocabulary will be needed. 

Actors:
Public user

Preconditions:
- None

Flow:
- Click on tile
- Select which info to display
- Visualize filtered predictions/results/info for tile

Exceptions:
- Impossible filter (combination) selection 

Postconditions:
- Visualization of filtered info
